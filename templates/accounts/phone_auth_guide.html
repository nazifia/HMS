Phone Number Authentication Guide

The HMS system now uses phone numbers for authentication. This guide explains how to use this feature.
Logging in with Phone Number

You can now log in to the HMS system using your registered phone number instead of your username. Simply enter your phone number in the login form and your password.

Example:
  • Phone Number: 9876543210
  • Password: [Your regular password]
Benefits of Phone Number Authentication
  • Easier to remember than usernames
  • More secure as phone numbers are unique
  • Faster login process
  • Enables SMS-based password recovery (coming soon)
Updating Your Phone Number

To update your phone number:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to your profile page
  3. Click on "Edit Profile"
  4. Update your phone number
  5. Save changes
Important: Make sure your phone number is unique and not used by another user in the system.
Fallback to Username

If you forget your phone number, you can still log in using your username as a fallback option.
